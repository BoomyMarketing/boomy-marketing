How long does it take to build a Toronto website?

A standard Toronto business website takes 4-6 weeks from strategy kickoff to launch. E-commerce builds take 8-12 weeks. Complex enterprise or custom-CMS projects run 10-14 weeks. We provide a detailed milestone timeline in your project proposal and keep you updated at every stage.
