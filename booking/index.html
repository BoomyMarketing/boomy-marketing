FAQ

Booking Questions

How do I book a consultation with Boomy Marketing?

Fill in the form on this page with your name, email, and a brief description of your project. We will respond within one business day to confirm your 30-minute strategy call. Alternatively, email us at care@boomymarketing.com or call (647) 528-9485.

What happens during a Boomy Marketing strategy call?

The call is a genuine conversation, not a sales pitch. We learn about your business, your current digital marketing, your goals and your challenges. Then we share our honest assessment of your biggest opportunities and what a realistic strategy would look like for your situation.

Is the strategy consultation free?

Yes. The initial 30-minute strategy call is completely free with no commitment required. We want to make sure we’re a good fit for each other before proposing anything.

How quickly will Boomy Marketing get back to me?

We respond to all booking enquiries within one business day. For urgent requests, call us directly at (647) 528-9485 and we’ll do our best to accommodate a same-day conversation.

Can I book a call if I’m not based in Toronto?

Absolutely. We work with clients across Canada and internationally. All consultations are conducted via video call (Google Meet or Zoom). Your location is no barrier to working with Boomy Marketing.
