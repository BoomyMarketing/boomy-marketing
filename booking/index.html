Who This Is For

Is a Strategy Session Right for You?

The Boomy Marketing strategy session is designed for Canadian business owners and marketing managers who want to improve their digital marketing results. It is most valuable for businesses that: currently have little or no organic traffic and want to understand why; are running Google Ads but want to reduce their dependence on paid traffic; had a website built recently and want to ensure it is performing as well as possible; are seeing their Google rankings decline and are not sure why; or want to understand how their digital marketing compares to their competitors.

The session is not appropriate for businesses looking for a quick fix or instant results. SEO is a long-term investment and we will tell you honestly what is realistic for your business in your market. If you are looking for an agency that will tell you what you want to hear rather than what is true, we are not the right fit. But if you want a genuine, evidence-based assessment of your growth potential and a credible strategy to achieve it, we would love to speak with you.

Before You Book

What to Expect from Your Free Discovery Call

Your discovery call with Boomy Marketing is a 30-minute conversation, not a sales pitch. We allocate equal time to understanding your business and goals as we do to explaining our services. Many clients leave the discovery call with useful strategic insights regardless of whether they proceed with us, because we do a genuine competitive and opportunity assessment during the call rather than reciting a service menu.

Before the call, it helps to have a general sense of your primary digital marketing goals. Are you trying to increase organic traffic and reduce dependency on paid advertising? Are you launching a new website or redesigning an existing one? Are you entering a new market or trying to grow market share in your existing geography? You do not need to have a fully formed brief: the call is designed to help clarify your objectives and priorities, not to confirm them.

We will ask about your current marketing spend and what it is producing. This is not to qualify you by budget but to understand your baseline ROI and identify where additional investment is most likely to generate above-average returns. A business spending $5,000/month on Google Ads with poor conversion tracking has a different opportunity profile than one with no current digital marketing investment. Both represent genuine opportunity, but the right starting point differs significantly.

We will review your current website and any Google Analytics or Google Search Console data you can share before the call. This preparation allows us to arrive with specific observations about your current performance rather than asking general questions that delay getting to useful insights. If you can share view access to your Google Analytics 4 property before the call, we will come prepared with data-driven talking points specific to your business.

After the call, if there is a mutual fit, we will send a proposal within two business days. Proposals include a specific scope of work, deliverables list, monthly investment, and projected outcomes based on our assessment of your market and competitive position. We do not send generic proposals: every proposal is written specifically for the business we have just spent 30 minutes understanding.
