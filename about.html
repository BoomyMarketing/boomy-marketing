📖 Our Origin

Built on the Belief That AI Changes Everything

Boomy Marketing was born at 240 Richmond St W, Toronto — right in the heart of the city's creative and tech district. Our founders had watched too many promising businesses burn through marketing budgets at traditional agencies that delivered thick reports, vague metrics, and zero accountability. By 2019, a pattern was clear: the agencies winning for their clients were the ones leveraging data and automation — not just creative talent.

In 2020, when AI tools became powerful enough to embed into real marketing workflows, we made a deliberate bet: build an agency from the ground up with AI at its core — not bolted on as an afterthought, but woven into every strategy, every campaign, and every deliverable. That first year we onboarded 40 clients across Toronto and proved the model worked: faster results, lower CAC, higher ROAS than anything they had seen from agencies 3x our price.

That bet paid off. By 2022, Boomy had grown to 150+ active clients and expanded beyond Toronto to serve businesses in Vancouver, Calgary, Ottawa, and Montreal. By 2025, we crossed 500 completed projects with a 98% client retention rate — a number we track obsessively because it tells us whether we're actually delivering. Today, Boomy Marketing is Canada's go-to agency for businesses that want to grow fast, stay ahead of the algorithm, and turn their marketing budget into a predictable revenue engine.

⚙️ How We Work

Our Process: From Audit
to Measurable Results

Every Boomy engagement follows the same four-phase methodology. No reinventing the wheel — just a proven system executed with precision.

1
Week 1–2
Deep Audit & Strategy

We audit your website, ad accounts, content, and competitors. We identify the highest-leverage opportunities — the ones that will move the needle fastest — and build a 90-day roadmap prioritised by expected ROI, not by what's easiest to do.

2
Month 1
Technical Foundation

We fix what's broken before building what's new. Technical SEO corrections, conversion rate optimisation on key pages, ad account restructuring, and tracking verification. This phase eliminates the leaks in your marketing funnel before we pour more budget in.

3
Month 2–3
Execution & Scaling

Content production, link-building campaigns, paid media scaling, email automation sequences, and AI outbound where applicable. This is where the plan becomes real and the first measurable gains start appearing in your analytics dashboard.

4
Month 4+
Optimise & Compound

Marketing results compound over time — but only if you keep improving. We analyse performance data monthly, A/B test continuously, expand what's working, and cut what's not. Most clients see their best results in months 4–6, after the foundation has had time to compound.
