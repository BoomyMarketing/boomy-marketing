📖 Our Origin

Built on the Belief That AI Changes Everything

Boomy Marketing was born at 240 Richmond St W, Toronto — right in the heart of the city's creative and tech district. Our founders had watched too many promising businesses spend tens of thousands on marketing agencies that delivered spreadsheets instead of results.

In 2020, when AI tools were just becoming powerful enough to use in real marketing workflows, we made a bet: build an agency from the ground up with AI at its core — not bolted on as an afterthought, but woven into every strategy, every campaign, and every deliverable.

That bet paid off. Today Boomy Marketing is Toronto's go-to agency for businesses that want to grow fast, stay ahead of the algorithm, and turn their marketing budget into a predictable revenue engine. We serve clients from downtown Toronto startups to national Canadian brands, all through the same AI-powered, data-first methodology.
