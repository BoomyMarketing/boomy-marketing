Why Graphic Design and Web Design Should Work Together

Many businesses make the mistake of hiring a graphic designer for branding and a separate agency for their website — then wonder why everything looks mismatched. Boomy Marketing handles both disciplines in-house, ensuring visual consistency across every touchpoint.

What Graphic Design Services Does Your Business Need?

Core graphic design deliverables for most Canadian businesses include: logo design, brand colour palette, typography system, business card and stationery design, social media templates, and digital ad creatives. These assets feed directly into the web design process, creating visual harmony across your online presence.

How Web Design Differs From Graphic Design

Graphic design is primarily visual communication — creating static or animated assets. Web design encompasses UX/UI, information architecture, responsive layouts, performance optimization, and conversion rate principles. A great website needs both: compelling visuals and smart structural design that guides visitors toward action.
