What Is Online Reputation Management Costs?

Online reputation management (ORM) involves monitoring, influencing, and improving how your business appears in search results and on review platforms. For Canadian businesses, a strong online reputation directly impacts customer trust, local search rankings, and revenue. A single-star increase in Google reviews can boost revenue by 5–9%.

Reputation management services in Canada range from CAD $500–$1,500/month for basic monitoring and review generation to CAD $3,000–$8,000/month for aggressive negative content suppression and comprehensive brand monitoring. Frequently Asked Questions

Can negative Google reviews be removed?

Reviews can be removed if they violate Google's policies (fake reviews, spam, conflict of interest). Legitimate negative reviews cannot be removed but can be responded to professionally and outweighed by new positive reviews.

How do I get more Google reviews for my Canadian business?

The most effective method is a systematic review request program — sending follow-up emails or SMS to customers after service delivery with a direct link to your Google review page. Boomy Marketing sets up and manages these programs for clients across Canada.

Is reputation management worth the cost for small businesses?

Yes. Given that reviews directly influence purchase decisions and local search rankings, investing CAD $500–$1,000/month in ORM typically delivers strong ROI for service-based Canadian businesses.

How does Boomy Marketing monitor online mentions?

We use enterprise brand monitoring tools that track mentions across Google, social media, news sites, forums, and review platforms in real time — alerting you to potential reputation issues before they escalate.

Can reputation management help with local SEO rankings?

Yes. Google uses review quantity, recency, and ratings as local ranking factors. A strong review generation program directly supports your local SEO performance.
