What Is What Is Drip Email Marketing?

What Is Drip Email Marketing is an automated email sequence that sends targeted messages based on subscriber behaviour or time intervals. For Canadian businesses, drip email marketing delivers 3x higher click-through rates than broadcast emails and generates CAD for every dollar spent — making it one of the highest-ROI digital marketing channels available.

How Drip Email Campaigns Work

Drip campaigns are triggered by specific actions: signing up for a newsletter, downloading a lead magnet, visiting a pricing page, or abandoning a cart. Each trigger initiates a pre-built sequence of emails delivered over days or weeks, nurturing the subscriber toward a specific goal — whether that's booking a call, making a purchase, or completing onboarding.

Types of Drip Email Campaigns for Canadian Businesses

Common drip sequences: welcome series (5–7 emails introducing your brand), lead nurture sequences (educating prospects over 2–4 weeks), post-purchase onboarding, re-engagement campaigns for inactive subscribers, and abandoned cart sequences for e-commerce. Boomy Marketing designs and builds custom drip campaigns optimized for Canadian audience behaviour.

Best Practices for Email Marketing in Canada (CASL Compliance)

Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) requires express or implied consent before sending commercial email. All emails must include a clear unsubscribe mechanism and your physical mailing address. Boomy Marketing ensures all email marketing campaigns are CASL-compliant by design — protecting your business from penalties while building subscriber trust.

How to Measure Drip Email Performance

Key email metrics: open rate (industry average 20–25%), click-through rate (2–5%), conversion rate (sequence-dependent), unsubscribe rate (should stay under 0.5%), and revenue per email sent. Boomy Marketing provides detailed email performance dashboards for all clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between drip emails and newsletters?

Newsletters are broadcast to your entire list on a schedule (weekly, monthly). Drip emails are automated sequences triggered by specific behaviour, making them highly relevant and timely — which is why they outperform newsletters in engagement and conversion metrics.

Which email platform should I use for drip campaigns?

Popular options for Canadian businesses include Mailchimp, Klaviyo (e-commerce), HubSpot, and ActiveCampaign. Platform choice depends on your CRM, list size, and automation complexity requirements.

How many emails should a drip sequence contain?

It depends on the goal. A welcome sequence typically has 5–7 emails over 2 weeks. A lead nurture sequence for B2B services might have 8–12 emails over 4–6 weeks. Start simpler and expand based on engagement data.

Is email marketing CASL-compliant automatically?

No. CASL compliance requires documented consent collection, proper unsubscribe mechanisms, and sender identification. Boomy Marketing builds CASL compliance into every email marketing setup we deliver.

What open rates should I expect from drip emails?

Well-segmented drip sequences typically achieve 30–50% open rates — significantly above broadcast email averages. The key driver is relevance: triggered emails arrive at the right moment in the subscriber's journey.
