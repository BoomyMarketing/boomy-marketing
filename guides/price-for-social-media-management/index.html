Understanding Price For Social Media Management for Canadian Businesses

Social media management involves planning, creating, scheduling, and analyzing content across platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, and TikTok. For Canadian businesses, effective social media management builds brand awareness, drives website traffic, and generates leads — when done with strategy and consistency.

What Does Social Media Management Cost in Canada?

Canadian social media management packages range from CAD $500/month for basic scheduling to CAD $4,000+/month for full-service management including content creation, community management, and paid social. Boomy Marketing's social media packages start at CAD $800/month and include strategy, content calendar, and monthly analytics reporting.

Which Social Platforms Should Canadian Businesses Prioritize?

Platform priority depends on your audience: LinkedIn dominates B2B in Canada, Instagram and Facebook lead for B2C retail and hospitality, TikTok is essential for reaching 18–35 year olds, and Google Business Profile posts support local SEO. Most Canadian SMBs should focus on 2–3 platforms rather than spreading thin across all channels.

What Does a Social Media Management Package Include?

A comprehensive social media management service covers: monthly content calendar, original graphics or video creation, caption writing, hashtag research, scheduled posting, community management (responding to comments/DMs), competitor monitoring, and monthly performance reports. Boomy Marketing includes all of these in our standard social packages.

How to Measure Social Media ROI

Track engagement rate (likes, comments, shares divided by reach), follower growth rate, website clicks from social, and social-attributed conversions in Google Analytics. Boomy Marketing integrates social analytics with your overall digital marketing dashboard so you can see the full picture.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many posts per week do I need on social media?

Quality beats quantity. For most Canadian businesses, 3–5 posts per week on primary platforms is more effective than daily posting of mediocre content. Consistency matters more than volume.

Should I use Hootsuite or Buffer for social media management?

Both are solid tools for scheduling. Hootsuite offers more analytics depth; Buffer is simpler for small teams. Boomy Marketing uses professional social media management suites that include these and additional analytics capabilities.

Do I need paid social ads in addition to organic management?

Organic reach on most platforms has declined significantly. We recommend allocating at least 20–30% of your social budget to paid promotion to amplify high-performing organic content and reach new audiences.

How long does it take to grow a social media following?

Organic growth takes 6–12 months of consistent posting to build meaningful follower counts. Paid campaigns can accelerate this. Boomy Marketing sets realistic growth benchmarks based on your industry and starting position.

Yes. We manage multi-location social media for Canadian franchise and chain businesses, including location-specific content strategies and consolidated reporting.
