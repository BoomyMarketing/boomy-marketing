What makes Canadian SEO different from US SEO?

Canadian SEO requires understanding of bilingual market dynamics, Canadian spelling conventions (colour vs color), regional search patterns across provinces, and Google's Canadian-specific index behaviour. Links from Canadian publications also carry more weight for Canadian keyword rankings than links from US-only domains.
