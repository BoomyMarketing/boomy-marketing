Frequently Asked Questions

What is E-E-A-T and how does it affect Canadian content?

E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) is Google's framework for evaluating content quality. Canadian businesses can demonstrate E-E-A-T through author bios with credentials, citing Statistics Canada and reputable Canadian sources, earning mentions in Canadian publications, and maintaining consistent factual accuracy across all content.

How much content does my Canadian business need to publish?

Research suggests that businesses publishing 2–4 blog posts per week generate 3.5x more traffic than those publishing once per week or less. However, quality and strategic alignment matter more than raw frequency. Boomy Marketing recommends a minimum of 4 pieces of optimised long-form content per month for meaningful SEO impact.

What type of content drives the most leads for Canadian businesses?

Case studies, comparison guides, and educational long-form content consistently drive the highest-quality B2B leads. For B2C, product reviews, how-to guides, and localised content perform best. Canadian buyers respond particularly well to content that addresses bilingual considerations and Canadian-specific context.

How do I measure content marketing ROI?

Content marketing ROI is measured through organic traffic growth, keyword ranking improvements, lead attribution (which pieces generate form fills and calls), and assisted conversion tracking in Google Analytics. Boomy Marketing sets up proper attribution tracking from day one of every content engagement.
