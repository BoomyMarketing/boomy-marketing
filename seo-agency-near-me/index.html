Frequently Asked Questions

What should I look for in an SEO agency near me?

Look for documented case studies with Canadian clients, transparent reporting practices, clear communication processes, and realistic timelines. Avoid agencies promising first-page results within 30 days or offering suspiciously low monthly rates under $1,000 CAD.

How long does SEO take to show results?

Initial technical improvements are visible within weeks. Keyword ranking improvements typically appear at 60–90 days. Significant organic traffic growth and ROI becomes measurable between months 6 and 12. Competitive markets may require 12–18 months for dominant first-page positioning.

Is it worth hiring a local SEO agency vs a national one?

Both can deliver excellent results. The advantage of local is market familiarity and easier collaboration. National agencies may bring broader resources. The key factor is their track record in your industry and their understanding of Canadian search behaviour.
