About the Site

Where can I find the Boomy Marketing XML sitemap?

The XML sitemap is at https://boomymarketing.com/sitemap.xml and is referenced in robots.txt. It is submitted to Google Search Console and updated whenever new pages are published.

How is the Boomy Marketing website organised?

The site is organised around four main areas: Services (SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, web design, content, AI automations), Local SEO pages (covering Canadian cities), SEO blog and guides (educational content for Canadian businesses), and company pages (about, pricing, contact, blog).

How do I find SEO services for my city?

Use the local SEO section in the site navigation or browse the city links on our SEO hub page at boomymarketing.com/seo/. We cover all major Canadian cities including Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, Kelowna, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Kitchener, Barrie, Richmond Hill, and Vaughan.

Can I request a page be added to the sitemap?

Contact us at care@boomymarketing.com if you are looking for digital marketing information or services for a specific Canadian city or industry that you cannot find on our site. We regularly add new location and service pages based on client needs.

How current is the Boomy Marketing website content?

We update our content regularly to reflect current digital marketing best practices, Google algorithm changes, and new Canadian market data. Our blog publishes new content 2-4 times per month. Core service pages are reviewed quarterly.

Service Locations

Where We Serve Canadian Businesses

Boomy Marketing provides digital marketing services to businesses across Canada, with particular depth of experience in Ontario, British Columbia, and Alberta markets. Our Toronto headquarters gives us direct knowledge of the GTA competitive landscape across all major industries including professional services, home services, healthcare, technology, retail, and hospitality.

For businesses in Vancouver and BC, we understand the Pacific Canadian market dynamics: the technology sector concentration in Vancouver, the tourism-driven economy in Whistler and Victoria, and the resource industry presence in northern BC that creates distinct search behaviour and competitive environments. Our BC-focused local pages cover Vancouver, Kelowna, Victoria, Burnaby, Surrey, and other major BC markets with locally-relevant competitive analysis.

Our Alberta campaigns serve businesses in Calgary and Edmonton across the energy sector, professional services, and the growing technology ecosystem. The Alberta market has distinct characteristics from Ontario: a different provincial competitive landscape, a resource-sector-influenced business culture, and search behaviour patterns that reflect the specific needs of Alberta consumers. Our Calgary and Edmonton local pages and service pages reflect this market specificity.

Our Services in Detail

Complete Digital Marketing Services for Canadian Businesses

Boomy Marketing provides a comprehensive suite of digital marketing services designed to work together as an integrated growth system. Each service is delivered by specialists with specific Canadian market expertise, and every campaign is built around measurable outcomes rather than activity metrics. We are a full-service digital marketing agency, which means you can consolidate all your digital marketing investment with a single accountable partner rather than coordinating between multiple specialist vendors.

Our Search Engine Optimisation service is the cornerstone of what we do. SEO provides the highest long-term return on investment of any digital marketing channel because rankings, once established, continue generating traffic without per-click costs. A strong organic presence reduces your dependency on paid advertising, lowers your blended customer acquisition cost, and provides a sustainable competitive advantage that compounds over time. We provide full-service SEO from technical audits through content production to link building, with transparent monthly reporting on rankings, traffic, and conversion outcomes.

Web design at Boomy Marketing means websites built from the ground up as revenue-generating tools. Every site we design is conversion-optimised from wireframe through to launch, SEO-ready with proper architecture and schema markup, and technically excellent with Core Web Vitals compliance and fast load times. We do not build websites that look good but generate no leads. Every design decision is evaluated against its impact on the business outcomes the site needs to produce.

Our paid advertising service provides immediate, measurable traffic while organic rankings are being built. We manage Google Search, Google Display, and Google Shopping campaigns for Canadian businesses across all budget levels. Campaign setup includes comprehensive conversion tracking so you always know the cost per lead or cost per acquisition from paid advertising, allowing accurate ROI calculation and informed budget decisions.

Content marketing builds the topical authority that makes all other digital marketing more effective. We produce SEO-optimised content that ranks for informational and commercial searches, builds E-E-A-T signals that improve overall domain authority, and provides prospects with the information they need to make confident purchase decisions. Our content is written by Canadian specialists with genuine subject matter knowledge, not generic content that could apply to any business anywhere.
