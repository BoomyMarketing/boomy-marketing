The Technology

What Is AI Outbound and Why It Outperforms Traditional SDRs

Traditional outbound sales development relies on human SDRs manually researching prospects, writing individual emails, and following up through spreadsheets. It is slow, expensive — a fully-loaded SDR in Toronto costs $80,000–$110,000 CAD per year — and highly inconsistent. Output depends entirely on the motivation and skill of each rep on any given day.

AI outbound changes this equation entirely. Our system uses large language models, intent data, and firmographic signals to identify prospects who match your Ideal Customer Profile precisely. It then generates personalised outreach at scale — emails that reference each prospect's company news, industry challenges, and LinkedIn activity — making every message feel individually crafted, even when delivered to thousands of contacts simultaneously.

The result is a pipeline-filling machine that operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week, learns from every reply, and continuously improves its own messaging and targeting. For Toronto B2B companies, this translates to a cost-per-meeting that is typically 60–80% lower than hiring a traditional SDR team.

Our Services

Everything Included in Our AI Outbound System

A complete outbound stack — from prospect identification to booked meeting — managed entirely by our team and AI.

🔍

AI Prospect Research & List Building

We build hyper-targeted prospect lists using AI-powered data enrichment tools, filtering by industry, company size, revenue, tech stack, hiring signals, and intent data. Every contact is validated and matched against your ICP before a single message is sent.

💌

Cold Email Sequences

Our AI writes multi-step cold email sequences with personalised first lines referencing each prospect's company, role, and recent activity. Sequences include strategic follow-ups, objection-handling emails, and breakup messages — all optimised for reply rates and CASL compliance.

👥

LinkedIn Automation

We automate LinkedIn connection requests, message sequences, and profile engagement to open a second outreach channel alongside email. Our LinkedIn outreach mirrors the personalisation of cold email — no generic copy-paste blasts that get ignored or flagged.

🤖

SDR Automation & AI Follow-Up

The AI handles all follow-up touchpoints automatically — detecting out-of-office replies, auto-routing warm responses to your sales team, and re-engaging cold prospects after set intervals. Your human reps only see prospects who have already expressed genuine interest.

📅

Meeting Booking & CRM Integration

Qualified prospects are routed directly to your Calendly, HubSpot, or Salesforce with full context on their company, conversation history, and engagement signals — so your closers walk into every call fully prepared and not starting from scratch.

📊

Performance Analytics & Optimisation

Weekly dashboards track open rates, reply rates, positive reply rates, meetings booked, and pipeline value generated. We use this data to A/B test subject lines, messaging angles, and ICP segments — continuously improving campaign performance month over month.

How It Works

From Setup to Booked Meetings in 2 Weeks

Our onboarding process is fast, structured, and designed to get your first campaign live within 14 days.

1

We deep-dive into your best customers to define your Ideal Customer Profile — industry, company size, title, pain points, and trigger events that predict a high likelihood to buy.

Campaign Launch

Campaigns go live with controlled sending volumes, real-time reply monitoring, and immediate routing of positive responses to your sales team within minutes of receipt.

Optimise & Scale

Weekly review of performance data drives continuous A/B testing, ICP refinement, and message improvement. Winning variations are scaled; underperformers are cut fast.
