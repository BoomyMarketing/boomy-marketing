Why Email Marketing

Why Email Marketing Is Still the Highest-ROI Channel in 2026

While social media algorithms limit organic reach to 1-5% of followers, email lands directly in your subscriber's inbox — a channel they chose to give you access to. The average email open rate of 20-35% dwarfs organic social engagement by 10-20x. And unlike paid advertising, your email list is an asset you own outright — no algorithm changes or platform policy shifts can take it away.

For e-commerce businesses, email typically drives 30-40% of total revenue. For service businesses, email nurture sequences convert leads who weren't ready to buy into clients months later — at zero incremental cost. For B2B companies, email is the most cost-effective way to stay top-of-mind with prospects through long sales cycles.

CASL Compliance

CASL-Compliant Email Marketing for Canadian Businesses

Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) is one of the world's strictest email marketing laws. Non-compliance can result in penalties up to $10 million per violation. Every email program we build is fully CASL-compliant from day one: proper consent collection and documentation, required sender identification, functional unsubscribe mechanisms, and suppression list management.

We're not just legally compliant — we help you build email programs based on genuine consent and audience value, which consistently outperform "spray and pray" approaches both ethically and commercially. CASL-compliant email programs tend to have higher engagement rates, better deliverability, and more loyal subscribers.
