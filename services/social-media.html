Our Content Approach

How We Create Content That Actually Gets Engagement

The fundamental mistake most businesses make with social media is treating it as a broadcasting channel for promotions. Audiences follow accounts that entertain, educate, or inspire them — not accounts that exist solely to push products. Our content strategy blends value-first content (that builds trust and grows your following) with strategic commercial content (that converts followers into customers) in ratios proven to maximise both reach and revenue.

Every piece of content we create starts with your audience's interests, pain points, and aspirations — and every content format is selected to maximise reach and engagement on the specific platform it's built for. A vertical Reel that works on Instagram requires a completely different approach than a LinkedIn thought leadership post or a TikTok trend-based video.

FAQ

Social Media Marketing Frequently Asked Questions

We manage Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube (including Shorts), Pinterest, and X. We'll recommend the right platform mix based on where your target audience actually spends time — not just popular platforms. Most clients focus on 2-3 platforms to build genuine depth rather than spreading thin across every channel simultaneously.
Posting frequency depends on your package and platform. Typical management includes 3-5 feed posts per week per platform plus daily or near-daily Stories on Instagram. We prioritise quality and consistency — a strong post 4 times per week outperforms a mediocre post every day, and the algorithm rewards accounts with consistent engagement, not just volume.
We handle end-to-end content creation — copywriting, graphic design, video editing, and caption writing. We work from your brand guidelines and do quarterly content briefings. If you have existing photography or video assets, we incorporate those into our content planning. You approve all content before it goes live through our collaborative approval system.
Organic social media growth takes time. Expect 3-6 months of consistent, quality content before meaningful audience growth and engagement benchmarks are established. Accounts with an existing base often see faster results. For faster growth, we recommend combining organic social management with paid Meta Ads and influencer partnerships — our integrated approach accelerates results significantly.
We track platform metrics (follower growth, reach, impressions, engagement rate, video views, saves and shares) alongside business outcomes (website clicks, lead form submissions, DMs). Monthly reports include all key metrics with trend analysis and strategic recommendations — benchmarked against your industry, not just your own historical performance.
