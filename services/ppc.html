Platform Coverage

PPC Platforms We Manage

Each platform has distinct strengths. We match your budget to the platforms where your customers actually spend time and money.

🔍

Google Ads

The world's largest PPC platform, capturing users at the highest point of purchase intent. We manage Search, Display, Shopping, YouTube, and Performance Max campaigns with rigorous keyword strategy, ad copy testing, and smart bidding frameworks. Google Ads remains the most effective paid channel for most Toronto businesses looking to generate leads or e-commerce revenue at scale. Every campaign is structured around Quality Score, conversion tracking, and measurable cost-per-acquisition targets.

📷

Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

With over 3 billion active users across Facebook and Instagram, Meta Ads deliver unmatched audience targeting capabilities based on demographics, interests, behaviours, and lookalike audiences. We build full-funnel Meta campaigns — from awareness video to conversion-focused retargeting — with creative-first strategies that stop the scroll and drive measurable action. Meta Ads are particularly powerful for B2C brands, e-commerce, and local service businesses reaching Toronto consumers.

💻

Microsoft Ads (Bing)

Microsoft Ads reaches over 900 million unique searchers globally, with a user base that skews older, more affluent, and more educated — a highly valuable audience for B2B, finance, legal, and home services. Average CPCs on Bing are typically 20-40% lower than Google for equivalent keywords, delivering better efficiency on your budget. We synchronise and independently optimise Microsoft Ads campaigns to capture intent-rich traffic your Google campaigns may miss entirely.

💼

LinkedIn Ads

LinkedIn is the premier platform for B2B paid advertising, enabling precise targeting by job title, company size, industry, seniority, and professional skills. We manage Sponsored Content, Message Ads, and Lead Gen Forms optimised for enterprise decision-makers and mid-market buyers. LinkedIn Ads command a higher CPC than other platforms, but the quality of leads generated for professional services and SaaS businesses consistently justifies the investment when campaigns are managed with the right objectives.

🎥

TikTok Ads

TikTok has become a dominant platform for brands targeting millennials and Gen Z, with video-first ad formats that drive both brand awareness and direct conversions. We develop creative-led TikTok campaigns using In-Feed Ads, TopView, and Spark Ads, working with your existing creative assets or guiding in-house production to create native-feeling content that performs. TikTok's growing Canadian user base and lower CPMs make it a cost-effective addition for consumer brands reaching the right audience demographic.

🌐

Programmatic Display

Programmatic advertising uses real-time bidding to place display and video ads across thousands of premium publisher sites, targeting your ideal audience wherever they browse online. We leverage programmatic platforms to run highly targeted retargeting, prospecting, and contextual campaigns that extend your paid reach far beyond the major search and social platforms. Programmatic is particularly effective for brand awareness campaigns, re-engaging high-value prospects, and reaching niche professional audiences at efficient CPMs.

Our Process

How We Build and Manage Your PPC Campaigns

A structured, data-driven management process that improves cross-platform performance month over month.

1

Audit & Discovery

We audit your existing paid accounts, ad spend history, and competitive landscape to uncover wasted budget, missed opportunities, and the platforms best aligned to your audience, goals, and budget.

2

Strategy & Platform Mix

We build a cross-platform PPC strategy with clear budget allocation across channels, defining campaign objectives, audience targeting frameworks, and bidding approaches tailored to your cost-per-acquisition targets and revenue goals.

3

Campaign Build

Our team builds every campaign from the ground up — keyword research, audience segmentation, ad copy creation, creative briefing, landing page alignment, and campaign architecture structured for maximum relevance scores and conversion performance.

4

Conversion Tracking

We implement comprehensive conversion tracking across all platforms before launch — including calls, form submissions, purchases, and micro-conversions — ensuring every budget decision is grounded in actual revenue data, not vanity metrics.

5

Continuous Optimisation

Weekly bid adjustments, audience exclusions, negative keyword refinement, A/B ad testing, creative rotation, and landing page iteration continuously reduce your cost-per-lead and improve ROAS across every active platform over time.

6

Transparent Reporting

Monthly performance reports connect ad spend directly to leads, revenue, and ROAS by platform — with clear attribution, trend analysis, and forward-looking recommendations. No confusing dashboards, just clarity on what's working.
