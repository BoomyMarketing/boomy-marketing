Campaign Types

Google Ads Campaign Types We Manage

Every campaign type serves a different purpose in your marketing funnel. We match the right format to the right objective.

🔍

Search Campaigns

Text ads that appear when users search for your products or services on Google. We conduct deep keyword research, write high-converting ad copy, structure campaigns for quality scores, and set smart bidding strategies that maximise conversions within your budget.

🖼️

Display & Remarketing

Visual banner ads shown across millions of websites in the Google Display Network. We use audience targeting and remarketing lists to keep your brand in front of past visitors and ideal prospects — converting window shoppers into buyers.

🛒

Google Shopping

Product listing ads for e-commerce businesses. We optimise your Google Merchant Center feed, product titles, descriptions, and bidding strategy to place your products in front of shoppers actively searching to buy — often the highest-converting ad format available.

🎥

YouTube Advertising

Video ads that build brand awareness, drive consideration, and capture demand. From skippable in-stream ads to YouTube Shorts, we develop video ad strategies that reach your target audience where they spend hours each week.

⚡

Performance Max

Google's AI-powered campaign type that runs across Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail, Discover, and Maps from a single campaign. We structure PMax campaigns with strong asset groups and audience signals to guide Google's AI toward your ideal customers.

🏠

Local Service Ads

Google-guaranteed ads for service-area businesses. Appear at the very top of Google Search results and pay only for qualified leads — not clicks. Ideal for home services, legal, healthcare, and other local service businesses in Toronto.

Our Process

How We Build and Manage Your Google Ads Campaigns

A rigorous, data-driven management process that improves performance month over month.

1

Account Audit

We audit your existing account (or build from scratch), identifying wasted spend, quality score issues, and missed opportunities across keywords, audiences, and ad formats.

2

Strategy & Structure

We design a campaign architecture aligned with your sales funnel — from awareness through conversion — with clear budget allocation and bidding strategies for each stage.

3

Ad Creation

Our copywriters craft compelling, benefit-driven ads with strong calls to action. We build multiple variants for A/B testing across headlines, descriptions, and extensions.

4

Launch & Tracking

We implement conversion tracking for every meaningful action — calls, form fills, purchases — so every campaign decision is backed by actual revenue data.

5

Ongoing Optimisation

Weekly bid adjustments, negative keyword refinement, ad rotation analysis, landing page testing, and audience optimisation continuously improve your cost-per-acquisition.

6

Monthly Reporting

Clear reports tie ad spend directly to leads, revenue, and ROAS — with strategic recommendations for the next 30 days.

Google Ads Frequently Asked Questions

For meaningful results in most Toronto markets, we recommend a minimum ad spend of $2,000-$3,000 CAD/month. Competitive industries like legal, real estate, and finance typically require $5,000-$15,000/month. Google Ads can generate leads and sales within the first week of launch. However, campaigns reach optimal performance after 30-60 days of data collection and optimisation. Our team refines targeting, bids, and ad copy continuously, so results consistently improve over the first three months.
