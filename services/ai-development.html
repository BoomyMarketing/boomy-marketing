Custom AI Development Services

From conversational AI to autonomous agents and data intelligence systems — we build what your business actually needs.

🤖

AI Chatbots & Assistants

Intelligent conversational agents for customer support, lead qualification, internal helpdesks, and e-commerce assistance. Built on GPT-4o, Claude, or Gemini with your company's knowledge base integrated via RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) for accurate, brand-consistent responses at any scale.

🧠

Autonomous AI Agents

Multi-step AI agents that can research, plan, and execute tasks across multiple tools and data sources without human oversight. We build agents for lead research and enrichment, competitive monitoring, content creation pipelines, automated customer outreach, and complex operational workflows.

📋

Document Intelligence

AI systems that read, extract, classify, and act on information from contracts, invoices, applications, emails, and unstructured documents. Extract structured data from PDFs, route documents to the right workflows, and automate compliance checks — eliminating hours of manual document processing.

📈

Recommendation Engines

Custom AI recommendation systems that personalise product suggestions, content feeds, pricing, and offers for individual users based on behaviour, preferences, and contextual signals. Increase average order value and engagement with ML-powered personalisation built specifically for your data.

🔗

API Integrations & Middleware

We build the connective tissue between AI capabilities and your existing software stack. Custom API layers that pipe AI intelligence into your CRM, marketing tools, e-commerce platform, ERP, or proprietary systems — enabling AI-powered features without rebuilding your technology infrastructure.

🧬

Model Fine-Tuning & RAG

When general AI models aren't specific enough, we fine-tune open-source models on your proprietary data or build RAG systems that ground AI responses in your authoritative knowledge base. Your AI speaks your brand's language, follows your processes, and knows your products and policies.

Our Process

How We Build Your Custom AI Solution

1

Discovery & Scoping

We define the problem clearly, assess feasibility, and determine the right AI approach — avoiding over-engineering simple problems with complex AI where automation suffices.

2

Architecture Design

We design the technical architecture: model selection, data flows, integration points, security model, and scalability approach before writing any code.

3

Prototype

A working prototype in 2-4 weeks demonstrates core functionality with real data. You see what the AI can do before committing to full development.

4

Build & Iterate

Full development with weekly progress reviews. We test with edge cases, refine prompts and model behaviour, and build robust error handling.

5

Integration & Testing

We integrate with your existing systems, run user acceptance testing, performance testing, and security review before deployment.

6

Deploy & Support

Production launch with monitoring, alerting, and ongoing support. As AI models evolve, we upgrade your solution to take advantage of new capabilities.
