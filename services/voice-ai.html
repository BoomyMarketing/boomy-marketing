The Problem We Solve

How Many Calls Is Your Business Missing Right Now?

Research shows that 85% of callers whose calls go unanswered will not call back — they'll simply contact a competitor. For most Toronto businesses, this represents enormous lost revenue. Yet hiring dedicated reception staff costs $45,000-$65,000 CAD per year per person, and even then, calls after hours, during lunch, and on weekends go unanswered.

AI voice agents solve this entirely. They answer every call instantly, handle the majority of inquiries completely autonomously, and seamlessly hand off to a human when genuinely needed — all for a fraction of the cost of even part-time reception staff. For businesses in healthcare, legal, real estate, home services, and hospitality, Voice AI is now a competitive necessity, not a luxury.

AI Inbound Receptionist

Appointment Booking Agent

AI that checks your real-time calendar availability and books appointments directly during the call — integrating with Calendly, Acuity, Google Calendar, and most scheduling systems. Sends confirmation and reminder messages automatically. Zero double-bookings, zero no-shows from poor communication.

Lead Qualification Agent

An AI that conducts a structured discovery conversation with inbound callers — asking qualification questions, scoring lead quality, and routing high-value opportunities to your sales team in real time while handling lower-priority inquiries autonomously. Never waste a salesperson's time on unqualified leads.

Outbound Voice Campaigns

AI-powered outbound calling for appointment reminders, payment follow-ups, customer satisfaction surveys, re-engagement campaigns, and event invitations. Reach hundreds of contacts per hour with personalised, natural-sounding conversations at a cost no human calling team can match.

After-Hours Coverage

Capture leads and serve customers outside business hours. Your AI agent handles all after-hours calls, collects contact details, logs inquiries in your CRM, and sends automatic summaries to your team each morning — so no opportunity is ever lost to a closed sign.

Call Analytics & Transcription

Every call is automatically transcribed, summarised, and logged to your CRM. Track call volume, common questions, booking rates, and sentiment trends. Use real caller data to continuously improve your products, services, and AI agent responses over time.

Our Process

How We Deploy Your AI Voice Agent

1

Discovery

We map your typical call types, common questions, escalation scenarios, and business rules — everything the AI needs to handle calls the way your best receptionist would.

2

Voice & Persona Design

We select the voice, accent, and conversational style that fits your brand. Your AI agent gets a name, personality, and tone guidelines aligned with how your business speaks.

3

Knowledge Base Build

We compile and structure your business information — services, pricing, hours, policies, FAQs — into the AI's knowledge base so it can answer questions accurately.

4

Integration Setup

We connect your AI agent to your phone system, CRM, calendar, and any other relevant tools. Call logs and bookings flow automatically into your existing workflow.

5

Testing & Refinement

We conduct dozens of test call scenarios — including edge cases and difficult callers — and refine responses until the agent performs to your satisfaction before going live.

6

Launch & Optimise

Go live with monitoring and weekly review of call transcripts during the first month. We continuously refine the agent based on real call data to improve performance over time.
