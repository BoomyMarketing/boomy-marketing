Your Next Steps with Boomy Marketing

Thank you for reaching out to Boomy Marketing. One of our team members will review your message and respond within one business day, usually within a few hours during business hours. We take every enquiry seriously and research your business and competitive landscape before our first conversation so we can provide informed, specific insights from the first interaction.

If you have submitted a booking request for a discovery call, you will receive a calendar invitation confirmation to your email. The discovery call is 30 minutes via Google Meet or Zoom. Before the call, it helps to have access to any Google Analytics or Google Search Console data you can share, along with a general sense of your primary goals for the next 12 months. This preparation allows us to arrive with specific observations about your current performance.

While you wait, explore the resources on our website that are most relevant to your situation. If you are evaluating SEO services, our SEO hub page covers our full service offering and methodology in detail. If you are considering a website redesign, our web design hub explains our process and what sets a conversion-optimised website apart from a typical agency build. Our blog contains in-depth guides on every aspect of digital marketing for Canadian businesses, written by our specialists based on real campaign experience.

Boomy Marketing has helped over 400 Canadian businesses grow their organic search presence since our founding in Toronto in 2020. We work with businesses of all sizes, from local Toronto shops to national e-commerce brands, across a wide range of industries. Whatever your digital marketing challenge, we are likely to have relevant experience and proven strategies to address it.

If you have any questions before our scheduled call, you are welcome to email us directly. We monitor our inbox during business hours Monday to Friday and respond to all messages promptly. We look forward to learning about your business and discussing how Boomy Marketing can help you achieve your digital marketing goals.

Our Promise

What Boomy Marketing Commits to Every Client

Every business that reaches out to Boomy Marketing receives the same level of serious attention regardless of the size of their enquiry. We do not tier our responsiveness by budget. A small local business exploring their first SEO investment gets the same quality of discovery conversation as an established brand evaluating a major campaign. We believe that the quality of our initial interaction is a reliable signal of how we treat clients throughout an engagement, and we design our intake process to demonstrate our values from the first contact.

We commit to honest advice, even when it is not what you want to hear. If your website has fundamental problems that would undermine any SEO campaign, we will tell you before you commit to a retainer. If your competitive environment requires a higher investment than your current budget allows to achieve meaningful results, we will tell you rather than accepting a budget we know is insufficient. If we do not believe we are the right fit for your specific needs, we will say so and where possible direct you toward a better match. This honesty is the foundation of the long-term relationships that define our business.

We measure our success by our clients' results, not by our own revenue. A client relationship that generates strong business outcomes and renews for years is more valuable to Boomy Marketing than a short engagement where we delivered the contracted scope but did not move the business needle. This alignment of incentives means every recommendation we make, every strategy we propose, and every campaign decision we take is evaluated against one question: does this produce better results for the client?
