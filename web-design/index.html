FAQ

Web Design Questions Answered

How much does a professional website cost in Canada?

Boomy Marketing’s web design packages start at $3,500 for a professional 5-page business website and go up to $15,000+ for custom e-commerce or enterprise builds. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements, goals and timeline.

How long does it take to build a website?

A standard business website takes 4–6 weeks from kickoff to launch. E-commerce and custom builds typically take 8–12 weeks. We provide a detailed project timeline during the discovery phase and keep you updated throughout.

Do you build mobile-friendly websites?

Every website Boomy Marketing builds is fully responsive and mobile-first. With over 60% of Canadian web traffic coming from mobile devices, mobile optimisation is not optional — it’s baked into our process from the first wireframe.

Will my website be optimised for SEO?

Yes. All Boomy Marketing websites are built with SEO best practices from the ground up: clean URL structure, optimised metadata, schema markup, fast load times, Core Web Vitals compliance and proper heading hierarchy. SEO is not an afterthought — it’s built in.

Which cities in Canada do you offer web design services?

We serve clients across Canada including Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, Ottawa, Mississauga, Edmonton, Southport and more. Our team works remotely and can deliver exceptional websites for any Canadian business regardless of location.
