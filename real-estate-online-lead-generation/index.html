Real Estate Online Lead Generation refers to the strategies and systems used to attract, capture, and qualify potential customers online. How to Measure Lead Generation ROI

Key metrics: Cost Per Lead (CPL), Lead Quality Score, Lead-to-Opportunity Rate, Opportunity-to-Close Rate, and Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC). Boomy Marketing implements proper attribution tracking so every lead is traced back to its source channel.

Common Lead Generation Mistakes to Avoid

The most costly mistakes: no lead scoring system, failure to follow up within 5 minutes of form submission, using generic landing pages instead of campaign-specific pages, ignoring mobile conversion optimization, and not A/B testing offer and copy variations. Frequently Asked Questions

How many leads should I expect per month from digital marketing?

Lead volume depends on your budget, industry, and conversion rate. A CAD $2,000/month Google Ads budget in a mid-competition market might generate 20–50 leads/month. Boomy Marketing sets realistic benchmarks based on your specific market.

What is a good cost per lead for Canadian businesses?

CPL benchmarks vary widely: home services CAD $30–$80, B2B services CAD $50–$200, real estate CAD $15–$50, healthcare CAD $40–$120. Boomy Marketing optimizes campaigns to achieve CPL targets based on your average client value.

What CRM tools does Boomy Marketing integrate with?

We integrate with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM, and most major CRM platforms. Proper CRM integration is essential for closing the loop between marketing spend and revenue.

How important is landing page design for lead generation?

Critical. A well-optimized landing page can convert 5–10% of visitors; a poor one converts under 1%. Boomy Marketing builds dedicated campaign landing pages for all lead generation clients.

Should I gate content to generate leads?

It depends on content value and funnel stage. Top-of-funnel educational content typically performs better ungated. Mid-funnel guides, templates, and tools can be gated to capture qualified leads willing to exchange contact details for value.
