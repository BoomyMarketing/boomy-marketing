What Does It Actually Mean to Up Your Digital Marketing?

For most Canadian businesses, "upping" their digital marketing means transitioning from isolated, reactive tactics to a cohesive, data-driven system that consistently generates leads and revenue. This is not about doing more of everything — it's about doing fewer things at a higher quality level and measuring each one rigorously. Businesses that up their digital marketing don't just run ads; they build an interconnected growth engine where each channel supports the others.

The five highest-impact moves we consistently see Canadian businesses make to improve their digital marketing results are: (1) fixing their SEO technical foundation, (2) unifying their paid and organic keyword data, (3) building a content calendar that serves both search and social, (4) implementing marketing automation to convert leads that would otherwise fall through the cracks, and (5) establishing proper attribution so they know exactly which channels drive revenue. Boomy Marketing helps with all five.

Why Is SEO the Foundation of Upping Your Digital Marketing?

Every other digital marketing channel benefits from a strong SEO foundation. When your website loads quickly, has clean structured data, and publishes high-quality content, your Google Ads Quality Scores improve, your social media link shares generate more engagement, and your email click-throughs land on pages that convert. SEO is not just about organic traffic — it disciplines your entire web presence toward quality and relevance.

According to HubSpot's 2024 marketing statistics, organic search generates over 1,000% more traffic than organic social media. For Canadian businesses debating where to allocate budget, this data point alone makes SEO investment non-negotiable as part of any serious digital marketing upgrade.

How Can AI Automation Help Canadian Businesses Up Their Digital Marketing?

AI-powered marketing automation addresses one of the biggest revenue leaks in Canadian digital marketing: slow lead follow-up. Research shows that responding to a new enquiry within 5 minutes is 100x more likely to result in a sale than waiting 30 minutes. Most Canadian businesses don't have the staff to respond to every lead within 5 minutes during business hours, let alone outside them. AI-driven chatbots and automated email sequences solve this problem completely.

Boomy Marketing's AI Automations service sets up lead capture, instant response, nurture sequences, and CRM integration that ensures every lead receives a personalised follow-up within seconds — regardless of when they submit a form or message your business. This single improvement typically increases lead-to-client conversion rates by 20–40% for Canadian service businesses without any additional advertising spend.

How Does Content Marketing Accelerate Every Digital Marketing Channel?

Content is the fuel that powers every digital marketing channel. Your Google Ads need landing page content to convert. Your SEO strategy needs blog posts and service pages to rank. Your email marketing needs valuable content to keep subscribers engaged. Your social media needs posts worth sharing. When all of these draw from a central content strategy — built around your audience's actual questions and search behaviour — you get exponentially more value from each piece produced.

Boomy Marketing builds content calendars that serve multiple channels simultaneously. A single well-researched blog post can rank organically for multiple keywords, be promoted via paid social, serve as a lead magnet email sequence, and provide source material for a month of social posts. This content multiplication approach gives Canadian businesses with limited marketing budgets the reach of a much larger organisation.

How Do You Measure Whether Your Digital Marketing Is Actually Improving?

The fundamental measurement framework for Canadian businesses upping their digital marketing is the same across all channels: reach (how many people are you reaching?), engagement (how many are engaging meaningfully?), conversion (how many are becoming leads or customers?), and revenue (how much are they worth?). Every channel should be tracked to at least the conversion level, and ideally to revenue attribution.

Boomy Marketing installs proper GA4 tracking, goal configuration, and call tracking from day one so that within 30 days of starting a campaign, you have data showing exactly which sources are driving your most valuable conversions. Most Canadian businesses we onboard discover that 1–2 channels are generating 80%+ of their results, and 2–3 others are consuming budget with minimal return. Reallocating that budget to top-performing channels is often the fastest way to up your digital marketing results. See Search Engine Land for current benchmarks on digital marketing performance.
