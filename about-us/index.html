Our Story

Why We Built Boomy Marketing

Boomy Marketing was founded in Toronto in 2020 by a team of digital marketers who had spent years watching Canadian businesses overpay for digital marketing that underdelivered. The common pattern was familiar: businesses spending thousands per month on Google Ads without building any lasting asset, or working with agencies that reported on impressions and clicks while revenue stayed flat.

We believed Canadian businesses deserved a different kind of agency. One that focused on building organic search authority as a lasting competitive asset, not just a short-term traffic source. One that reported on leads, revenue, and ROI rather than vanity metrics. One that worked exclusively in the Canadian market and understood the specific competitive landscape of every city from Vancouver to Halifax.

Six years later, Boomy Marketing has worked with over 400 Canadian businesses across virtually every industry and province. Our clients include local service businesses, national e-commerce brands, professional services firms, technology companies, and retail chains. What they share is a commitment to building digital growth that compounds over time, not campaigns that spike and disappear.

Our headquarters at 240 Richmond St W in Toronto's downtown core puts us at the heart of Canada's largest business market. But we work with clients across the country, from Victoria to St. John's, running campaigns in every major Canadian market. Our team combines expertise in SEO, paid media, web design, content, and AI automation, covering every channel needed to build a complete digital marketing programme.

Our Services

How We Help Canadian Businesses Grow

Boomy Marketing is a full-service digital marketing agency. Our core service lines cover every channel a Canadian business needs to build a complete online presence: SEO for organic traffic, Google Ads for immediate paid visibility, Meta Ads for social media advertising, web design for conversion-optimised websites, content marketing for authority building, and AI automations for operational efficiency.

Most clients start with one or two channels and expand their programme as initial results demonstrate ROI. A typical growth trajectory starts with SEO and Google Ads simultaneously: Google Ads generates immediate leads while SEO builds the organic foundation. Over 12-18 months, as organic rankings grow, clients often reduce their Ads spend as organic traffic delivers the same lead volume at lower cost.

Our approach is always integrated. SEO insights inform content marketing topics. Content marketing produces the pages that earn backlinks and support SEO authority. Paid media data reveals which messaging converts best, which improves organic CTR when applied to title tags and meta descriptions. Web design choices affect Core Web Vitals scores which affect SEO rankings. Every channel is more effective when it is coordinated with the others.

Our Philosophy

How Boomy Marketing Thinks About Digital Marketing

The digital marketing industry has a trust problem. Agencies promise results they cannot deliver, use jargon to obscure underperformance, and optimise for client retention rather than client success. Many Canadian businesses have been burned by agencies that charged premium fees for minimal results, then blamed algorithm changes, competitive markets, or insufficient client content when campaigns underdelivered. Boomy Marketing was built as a direct response to this pattern.

Our founding principle is accountability to outcomes that matter to your business: leads, revenue, and return on investment. Not impressions, not clicks, not rankings for keywords nobody searches for. Before we begin any campaign, we establish baseline metrics for the outcomes you care about and build a measurement framework that connects our work to those outcomes. When we report monthly, we report on your business results, not on our activity.

We believe in education over mystification. Many agencies treat their methodology as a black box, using proprietary frameworks and opaque reporting to prevent clients from evaluating the real value being delivered. We take the opposite approach: we explain exactly what we are doing and why, we share the data we are acting on, and we teach our clients enough about digital marketing to be informed buyers. Clients who understand what we are doing get more value from our work because they can provide better strategic input and evaluate our recommendations critically.

We only work with clients we believe we can genuinely help. This means our initial consultation is a two-way evaluation: we are assessing whether your business, budget, and goals are a genuine fit for our services, just as you are evaluating us. We turn away clients where we do not believe our services can deliver meaningful ROI, rather than taking every project and delivering mediocre results. This selectivity is why our client retention rate exceeds 85% after twelve months.

Our team is based in Toronto and works exclusively on Canadian market clients. We do not outsource strategy, content, or link building to overseas contractors. We use offshore development resources for some technical tasks under close internal supervision, but all client-facing work and strategic decision-making happens within our Toronto-based team. This Canadian focus means our team understands the specific competitive dynamics, consumer behaviour patterns, and regional nuances that determine success in Canadian digital marketing.
