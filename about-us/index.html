Who We Are

Boomy Marketing was built on a simple belief: digital marketing should be transparent, measurable, and directly tied to business revenue. Since launching in Toronto in 2020, we’ve helped over 250 Canadian businesses grow their online presence through SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, web design and AI-driven automation.

We are not a faceless agency that outsources your work overseas. Every campaign is run by our in-house team of strategists, content writers, and technical SEO specialists who care about your results as much as you do.

Our Philosophy

We believe in doing less, better. Rather than spreading ourselves thin across every possible marketing channel, we focus on the strategies that move the needle: organic search, paid media, and conversion optimisation. We measure everything, optimise constantly, and report transparently.

We work with businesses at every stage — from local Toronto shops just starting with digital marketing to national brands looking to scale their paid media spend and enterprise companies automating their lead generation with AI.

Our Services

Boomy Marketing offers a full suite of digital marketing services designed to work together as an integrated growth system:

  • SEO — Technical SEO, content strategy, link building and local SEO across Canada
  • Google Ads — Search, Shopping, Display and Performance Max campaigns
  • Meta Ads — Facebook and Instagram advertising for lead generation and e-commerce
  • Web Design — Fast, SEO-ready websites built to convert visitors into customers
  • AI Automations — AI-powered workflows that reduce manual work and scale outreach
  • Content Marketing — Strategic content that builds authority and drives organic growth

FAQ

About Boomy Marketing

When was Boomy Marketing founded?

Boomy Marketing was founded in 2020 in Toronto, Ontario. Since then we’ve grown into a full-service digital marketing agency serving clients across Canada and internationally, with a core focus on SEO, paid media, and AI-driven marketing automation.

Where is Boomy Marketing located?

Our team is based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. We work with clients across Canada and internationally, operating as a remote-first agency so we can serve businesses in every province without geographic limitations.

How many clients does Boomy Marketing work with?

We work with 250+ active clients ranging from local Toronto service businesses to national e-commerce brands and funded SaaS companies. Our team size stays intentionally lean to maintain quality and personalised attention on every account.

What industries does Boomy Marketing specialise in?

We work across many verticals including e-commerce, B2B SaaS, professional services, healthcare, real estate, hospitality and local service businesses. Our data-driven approach adapts to any industry where organic search and paid media can drive growth.

How do I get started with Boomy Marketing?

Simply reach out via our contact page or call (647) 528-9485. We start with a free 30-minute strategy call to understand your goals, followed by a complimentary audit of your current digital presence. From there we build a custom roadmap and proposal.
