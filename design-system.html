Most clients see measurable organic traffic growth within 3–6 months. Competitive markets like Toronto may take 6–9 months for significant ranking improvements, but quick wins (featured snippets, long-tail keywords) often appear within the first 60 days.
Do you work with small businesses?
Absolutely. Our Starter plan at $1,500 CAD/mo is designed specifically for growing SMBs ready to invest in sustainable, long-term organic growth without enterprise-level spend.
Is there a contract lock-in period?
No long-term contracts. We operate month-to-month because we believe the results should earn your continued partnership — not legal obligation.
