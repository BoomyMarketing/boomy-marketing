What We Offer

Every Digital Marketing Channel, One Canadian Agency

Boomy Marketing is a full-service Canadian digital marketing agency headquartered in Toronto and working with clients across all provinces. Since our founding in 2020, we have built a team with deep expertise across every major digital marketing channel: SEO, paid search, paid social, web design, content marketing, and AI automation.

The integrated approach we take means every channel is more effective because it is coordinated with the others. SEO insights inform content topics. Content creates pages that earn links and support SEO authority. Paid media data reveals which messages convert, improving organic CTR. Website design choices affect Core Web Vitals scores which affect rankings. When these channels work together rather than in silos, the total impact is significantly greater than the sum of the parts.

Most Canadian businesses start with one or two channels and expand as results demonstrate ROI. A common growth path: SEO and Google Ads launch simultaneously, generating both immediate (paid) and compounding (organic) traffic. As SEO matures over 12-18 months, organic takes over more of the lead volume and paid budgets can be reduced or redirected to growth rather than maintenance. Web design and content support both channels throughout.

Service Integration

How Our Services Work Together

The most effective digital marketing strategies integrate multiple channels in a coordinated programme rather than running each service in isolation. SEO and paid advertising complement each other: paid ads provide immediate traffic while organic rankings are being built, and organic ranking data informs which keywords and audiences are most valuable to target in paid campaigns. Web design and SEO are inseparable: a technically excellent website is the foundation that makes all other digital marketing more effective and more efficient.

For Canadian businesses starting from a limited digital marketing footprint, the typical integration sequence is: website performance foundation first (ensuring the site converts traffic effectively and has clean SEO architecture), then local SEO to capture immediate high-intent local searches, then content marketing to build topical authority, then national SEO to compete for broader market keywords. Paid advertising can be layered in at any point to accelerate traffic to priority pages while organic rankings develop.

E-commerce businesses have a distinct integration priority: technical SEO and site architecture must be resolved before significant content investment makes sense, because structural issues like duplicate content from faceted navigation, incorrect canonical implementation, and poor internal link architecture can cause Google to devalue all content on the site. Boomy Marketing performs an e-commerce technical audit before beginning any content or link building work for online retail clients.

Professional service businesses (legal, accounting, consulting, healthcare, insurance) benefit most from the combination of local SEO, authoritative content marketing, and conversion-optimised landing pages. The buying journey for professional services involves significant research: a prospective client typically visits multiple pages, reads multiple articles, and compares multiple providers before making contact. A content-rich website that ranks for research-phase keywords as well as commercial keywords captures prospects earlier in the journey and builds the authority that makes conversion more likely.

Seasonal businesses need to plan digital marketing campaigns that peak at the right time. SEO takes time to build: a landscaping company that starts an SEO campaign in April will not see full ranking impact until the following spring season. Boomy Marketing builds campaign calendars for seasonal businesses that front-load SEO work to ensure rankings are established before peak demand periods, supplemented with paid advertising that can be turned on immediately when needed.
