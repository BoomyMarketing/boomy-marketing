The 7 Criteria for Choosing Canada Best Digital Marketing Agency

Apply these seven criteria to any Canadian digital marketing agency you evaluate, including Boomy Marketing. If a prospective agency cannot satisfy all seven, approach with appropriate caution.

  • Google Certified or Partner status - Verifiable proof of platform expertise. Check the Google Partner directory directly.
  • Canadian client references in your industry - Relevance to your specific market matters enormously.
  • Transparent CAD pricing with itemised deliverables - No currency conversion surprises, no vague strategy charges.
  • Ethical methodology documentation - Can they explain their link-building strategy in plain language?
  • Integrated multi-channel capability - SEO, paid ads, and content managed with shared data, not siloed.
  • Business-outcome reporting - Reports connecting to leads, revenue, and ROI, not just clicks and impressions.
  • No lock-in contracts - Top agencies retain clients through results, not legal terms.

What Best Practices Should a Top Canadian Digital Marketing Agency Follow?

Best practices Canadian agencies must follow: content is always original and Canadian-audience-relevant; links are earned through genuine editorial outreach, never purchased; paid ad campaigns are reviewed weekly rather than monthly; reporting is transparent and outcome-focused; strategy is updated quarterly based on performance data, not renewed on autopilot year after year.
