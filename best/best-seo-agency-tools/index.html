What Do the Best SEO Agencies Have in Common?

The best SEO agencies in any market share several characteristics: they prioritise technical SEO fundamentals before content, they build links through genuine outreach (not link farms), they produce content that earns citations rather than just word count, and they measure success through revenue-correlated metrics — organic leads, organic revenue, and share of voice — not just keyword rankings.

They also invest in their own organic presence. An SEO agency that cannot rank its own website for competitive terms is a significant red flag.

How to Evaluate an SEO Agency Before Hiring

Request a sample technical audit of your website. Ask for a link-building case study with domain authority progression documented. Ask how they handle algorithm updates — the best agencies have contingency protocols, not just reactive scrambling. Verify that they follow Google's Webmaster Quality Guidelines and ask specifically about their stance on AI-generated content, which Google has updated its position on multiple times since 2023.

What Results Can You Expect From a Top SEO Agency?

Realistic expectations from a top SEO agency: 30–80% organic traffic growth in 6–12 months for an established domain, 3–6 months to see initial movement for a new site, and 12–18 months to achieve sustained top-3 rankings for competitive keywords. Any agency promising page one rankings within 30 days for competitive terms is either lying or using tactics that will eventually result in a Google penalty.

What Is the Difference Between Local SEO and National SEO?

Local SEO targets searches with geographic intent ("SEO agency Toronto", "digital marketing near me") and involves Google Business Profile optimisation, local citations, and locally-relevant content. National SEO targets broader non-geographic terms and requires domain authority building through content marketing and link acquisition. The best agencies excel at both — and understand how local signals feed into national rankings.

How Much Does a Top SEO Agency Charge in Canada?

Top Canadian SEO agencies charge CAD $1,500–$6,000/month for comprehensive SEO programs. Project-based work (technical audits, site migrations) ranges from $2,500–$15,000. Be cautious of packages under $800/month — quality SEO requires significant labour hours and cannot be delivered profitably at low price points. Always ask for an itemised scope of work with hours allocated to each activity.
