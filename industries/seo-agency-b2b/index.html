Why SaaS and B2B Marketing Requires Specialist Expertise

B2B purchase decisions in Canada involve 5–8 stakeholders on average and take 3–9 months. Marketing must address different objections at each stage of the buying committee and the buyer journey. Generic marketing agencies build awareness campaigns — B2B specialists build full-funnel programs that nurture leads through every stage of the pipeline, with content and paid tactics mapped to each decision-maker's specific concerns.

What Digital Marketing Channels Work Best for SaaS in Canada?

For Canadian SaaS companies, the highest-ROI channels are: LinkedIn for reaching decision-makers with targeted content and InMail campaigns, Google Search for capturing high-intent searches from buyers actively evaluating solutions, content marketing for organic lead generation over 12–18 months, email nurture for converting trials and leads, and G2/Capterra review management for social proof at the evaluation stage. The channel mix shifts significantly by stage — early-stage startups prioritise content and LinkedIn; growth-stage companies invest heavily in paid acquisition and conversion rate optimisation.

How to Build a Content Marketing Strategy for SaaS

Effective SaaS content marketing in Canada follows the jobs-to-be-done framework: identify the problems your buyers are trying to solve, create content that helps them solve those problems better than any competitor, and build topical authority through interconnected content clusters. Case studies with quantified ROI data are the single highest-converting content type for B2B SaaS across all funnel stages.

What Lead Generation Strategies Work for B2B and SaaS?

For Canadian B2B companies: LinkedIn thought leadership (personal profiles outperform company pages by 3–5x), webinar lead generation, high-value content upgrades (benchmarking reports, calculators, templates), partner and integration marketing, and account-based marketing for enterprise targets. Boomy Marketing builds ABM programs targeting named accounts in Canada's major markets — particularly Toronto's Bay Street corridor and Vancouver's technology sector.

How Do You Measure Marketing ROI for SaaS and B2B Companies?

Key SaaS marketing metrics: Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by channel, CAC Payback Period, Marketing Qualified Lead (MQL) to SQL conversion rate, pipeline contribution by channel (using multi-touch attribution), and Net Revenue Retention for cohorts acquired through different channels. Monthly reporting should track the full funnel from impression to closed-won revenue, not just top-of-funnel vanity metrics.
