What Digital Marketing Channels Work Best for Canadian Real Estate?

Facebook and Instagram advertising remain the highest-volume channels for real estate lead generation in Canada — primarily because of precise geographic and demographic targeting and the visual nature of property content. Google Search captures active buyers and sellers with high intent. SEO for real estate is a longer play but delivers the lowest cost per lead once established. YouTube virtual tours have grown significantly since 2022, particularly for luxury properties and new developments.

How to Generate Real Estate Leads with Facebook Advertising

Effective Facebook Ads for Canadian realtors combine geographic radius targeting (typically 10–25km around your active markets), detailed demographic filters (household income, homeowner status, life events like "newly engaged" or "recently moved"), and creative that showcases properties with professional photography or video. Lead generation ads (with forms pre-filled from Facebook profile data) consistently outperform link ads for volume; link ads typically deliver higher intent. Split testing both is the right approach.

What Is Social Media Marketing Best Practice for Real Estate?

The best social media approach for Canadian real estate: post property showcases (photos, video tours, neighbourhood guides) 4–5x per week, mix in market data content (average prices, days on market, absorption rates), share client success stories with proper consent, and maintain consistent personal branding that emphasises your local expertise. Short-form video (Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts) dramatically outperforms static images for organic reach in 2026.

How Does Local SEO Help Real Estate Professionals?

Local SEO for real estate targets searches like "Toronto realtor", "homes for sale in Mississauga", and "real estate agent Vaughan". Ranking for these terms puts you in front of buyers and sellers at the moment of highest intent. Key tactics: Google Business Profile optimisation with neighbourhood-specific content, neighbourhood landing pages on your website, and listings schema markup. A well-optimised real estate website can generate 15–30 organic inquiries per month from local search alone.

What Does Real Estate Digital Marketing Cost in Canada?

Individual realtors typically invest CAD $1,500–$3,000/month in digital marketing (Facebook Ads + basic SEO + social media). Team leads and small brokerages invest $3,000–$8,000/month. Developers running pre-construction marketing campaigns often invest $15,000–$50,000/month during active sales periods. Marketing ROI for real estate is highly measurable — a single commission from a digitally acquired lead typically justifies several months of marketing spend.
