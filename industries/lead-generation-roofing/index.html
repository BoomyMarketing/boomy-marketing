What Digital Marketing Channels Generate the Most Leads for Home Services?

Google Local Services Ads (LSAs) are the single highest-converting channel for home service businesses in Canada. They appear above traditional paid search results, show your Google rating prominently, and charge per verified lead rather than per click. Combined with Google Search Ads for coverage of terms LSAs miss, SEO for non-branded searches, and a well-optimised Google Business Profile, these three channels typically generate 80% of leads for home service businesses.

How to Use SEO to Generate Leads for Roofing and HVAC Companies

Service-area pages are the foundation of home service SEO. A roofing company serving the GTA should have dedicated pages for each community they serve (Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, etc.) that contain genuine local content — not just the company name and city swapped in. Each page should have schema markup identifying the service area, local testimonials, and a click-to-call phone number that is trackable. This structure can deliver 50–200 organic leads per month for a well-established company in a major Canadian market.

What Makes a Roofing or HVAC Website Convert Visitors Into Leads?

The highest-converting home service websites in Canada share these features: phone number prominently displayed in the header (click-to-call on mobile), trust signals above the fold (Google rating, BBB badge, years in business), a short contact form with 3 fields or fewer, fast load speed (under 2.5 seconds on mobile), and a gallery of recent work with before/after photography. Conversion rate optimisation alone can double the leads you get from your existing traffic.

How Do You Measure Lead Generation Performance for Home Services?

Track calls from each channel using call tracking numbers (CallRail is the most common tool in Canada), form submissions with source attribution, and close rate by lead source to calculate true cost per acquired job. Many home service companies track cost per lead but not cost per booked job — this misses significant information. A Google LSA lead might cost $30 while a Facebook lead costs $15, but if LSA leads convert at 35% and Facebook leads at 10%, LSA delivers the better economics.

What Does Lead Generation Cost for Home Services in Canada?

For roofing and HVAC companies in Canadian urban markets, well-managed digital marketing programs deliver leads at CAD $30–$80 per verified inquiry. Google LSAs charge per lead ($20–$60 depending on service type and market). Total monthly marketing investment for a single-market home service business typically ranges from $2,000–$5,000/month including ad spend and management fees.

What is Google Local Services Ads and how does it work?
Google LSAs are pay-per-lead ads that appear at the very top of Google search results for service searches. Google verifies your business license and insurance before approving your listing. You are charged only when a customer calls or messages you through the ad.

Initial ranking improvements appear within 60–90 days of technical SEO work. Service-area pages typically reach the first page of local results within 3–6 months. Competitive markets like the GTA may take 6–12 months for top-3 rankings on high-value keywords.
