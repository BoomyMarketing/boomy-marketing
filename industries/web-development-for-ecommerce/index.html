What Digital Marketing Channels Drive the Most E-Commerce Revenue in Canada?

For most Canadian e-commerce businesses, the revenue-generating channel hierarchy is: Google Shopping and Performance Max (highest purchase intent), Meta Ads (broadest audience reach with strong retargeting), email marketing (highest ROI for existing customer base), SEO (lowest long-term cost per acquisition), and organic social (lowest direct conversion but strongest brand building). The optimal channel mix depends on your average order value, product category, and target customer demographics.

How to Set Up Google Shopping for Canadian E-Commerce

Google Shopping requires a Google Merchant Center account with your product feed correctly configured with Canadian pricing (CAD), accurate availability status, and GTIN or MPN numbers. Product titles should front-load the highest-search-volume keywords while remaining natural. Performance Max campaigns have replaced Smart Shopping — configure asset groups by product category and supply high-quality creative assets. Enable price competitiveness insights to understand how your pricing compares in Canadian auctions.

What Is the Best Meta Ads Strategy for E-Commerce?

For Canadian e-commerce, the proven Meta Ads structure is: a top-of-funnel broad audience campaign with the highest-quality creative (video performs best), a mid-funnel retargeting campaign targeting website visitors and video viewers, and a bottom-funnel dynamic product ad campaign targeting add-to-cart abandoners and past purchasers with complementary products. ROAS targets should be set at the account level, not the campaign level, to allow Meta's algorithm to optimise cross-campaign.

How Do You Build a High-Converting E-Commerce Email Program?

Essential email flows for Canadian e-commerce: welcome series (3–5 emails over 10 days for new subscribers), abandoned cart flow (3 emails over 48 hours), browse abandonment flow (1–2 emails), post-purchase flow (product education, review request, cross-sell), and win-back flow for lapsed customers. Together these automated flows can generate 25–35% of total email revenue with no ongoing creative effort. Klaviyo is the recommended platform for Shopify stores; integration with your store data is essential for personalised product recommendations.

What Is a Good ROAS Target for Canadian E-Commerce Advertising?

Canadian e-commerce ROAS benchmarks vary significantly by category: fashion averages 3–5x, health and beauty 4–7x, home goods 2–4x, electronics 2–3x, and specialty niche products can achieve 6–10x. These are advertising ROAS figures — overall marketing ROAS (including email, SEO, etc.) should be evaluated separately. A 4x blended ROAS on a 40% gross margin product means you are spending $0.10 to generate $0.40 in gross profit — healthy economics that warrant investment.

What is Performance Max and should Canadian e-commerce brands use it?
Performance Max is Google's AI-driven campaign type that serves ads across Search, Shopping, Display, YouTube, Gmail, and Maps. For most e-commerce brands with sufficient conversion data, PMax delivers better ROAS than manually managed Shopping campaigns. We recommend it with proper asset group structure and audience signals.
How does email marketing work with CASL for e-commerce?
E-commerce businesses can send marketing emails to customers who have made a purchase within the past 24 months under CASL's implied consent provisions. For new subscribers who have not purchased, express consent is required. We build CASL-compliant consent flows and sunset sequences into all e-commerce email programs.
What is a realistic timeline to see results from e-commerce digital marketing?
Google Shopping and Meta Ads deliver measurable results within 30–60 days of launch. Email automation delivers ROI within 60–90 days of setup. SEO delivers meaningful organic revenue growth within 6–12 months.
Do you offer conversion rate optimisation (CRO) for e-commerce?
Yes. Our e-commerce programs include landing page and product page CRO using A/B testing, heatmap analysis, and session recordings. CRO typically improves conversion rates by 15–40%, which amplifies the ROI of every other marketing channel.
