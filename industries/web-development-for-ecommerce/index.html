What Are the Compliance and Regulatory Considerations for Web Development For Ecommerce Marketing in Canada?

Canadian web development for ecommerce businesses marketing digitally must navigate several regulatory considerations. CASL governs all commercial electronic communications — email and SMS outreach require documented consent. Industry-specific advertising standards may apply depending on the vertical: financial services face IIROC and OSC standards, healthcare providers face College advertising guidelines, legal services face Law Society restrictions. PIPEDA and Quebec Law 25 govern data collection and processing. A non-compliant campaign in a regulated web development for ecommerce sector carries reputational and legal risk that far exceeds any short-term performance benefit.
