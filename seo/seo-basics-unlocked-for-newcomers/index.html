SEO Basics

SEO Basics Unlocked for Newcomers

This guide breaks down SEO into its simplest most actionable components so newcomers can start improving their website search visibility right away.

Published 2023-09-28Boomy Marketing

Start With the Search Engine Perspective

To understand SEO, think like a search engine. Google's mission is to provide the most helpful, relevant, and trustworthy answer to every search query. When Googlebot crawls your website, it asks: What is this page about? Is it the most helpful page on this topic? Can I trust the information? Is it a good experience on all devices? Your job is to make every one of those questions easy to answer in your favour.

Your Most Important Free SEO Tools

Google Search Console shows which queries bring people to your site, which pages are indexed, and any technical errors. Google Analytics tracks traffic, user behaviour, and conversions. Google Keyword Planner shows search volume and competition for any keyword. Google PageSpeed Insights analyses your page speed with specific fix recommendations. Google Business Profile is the most important local SEO tool for businesses serving a specific area. All five tools are completely free.

The Newcomer 30-Day SEO Action Plan

Week 1 - Setup and Audit: Set up Google Search Console and Google Analytics. Check which pages are indexed. Identify critical technical errors. Claim your Google Business Profile. Week 2 - Keyword Research: Identify 10-20 keywords your customers search for. Focus on local terms, specific service terms, and questions customers ask. Week 3 - On-Page Optimisation: Update title tags, meta descriptions, and H1 headings on your most important pages to include target keywords. Week 4 - Content and Links: Add or improve content on key pages to at least 500-800 words of genuine value. Claim 5-10 free business directory listings to build initial backlinks.

What to Expect After 30 Days

After 30 days of consistent implementation you will have a technically healthier website, pages optimised for specific keywords, a foundation of local citations, and visibility into what Google sees when it crawls your site. You will not see dramatic ranking changes yet since SEO takes time to compound. But you will have laid the foundation that everything else builds on. Months 3-6 is when results typically become clearly visible in Search Console rankings data.

FAQ

Common Questions

How difficult is SEO to learn?

The basics are accessible to anyone with basic computer literacy and willingness to learn. Advanced technical SEO requires more expertise. Start with Google documentation and free courses.

What is a backlink and why does it matter?

A backlink is a link from another website pointing to yours. Google treats backlinks as votes of confidence. A link from a respected site carries significant authority and improves your rankings.

How do I get my website to appear in Google Maps?

Claim and fully optimise your Google Business Profile at business.google.com. Fill in all information, choose correct business categories, and gather customer reviews.

What is a meta description?

The 2-3 sentence summary appearing under your page title in Google results. It does not directly affect ranking but a well-written meta description improves click-through rate. Keep it under 160 characters.

Is blogging important for SEO?

Yes. Each blog post is a new page that can rank for additional keywords. A consistent blogging strategy targeting customer questions creates topical authority and attracts backlinks that compound in value.
