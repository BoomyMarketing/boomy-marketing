How Does SEO Support Paid Digital Marketing Campaigns in Canada?

One of the most underappreciated benefits of SEO for Canadian businesses running paid ads is Quality Score improvement. When your landing pages are well-optimised for both users and search engines, Google rewards you with higher Quality Scores on your Google Ads campaigns — translating directly into lower cost-per-click and better ad placement. This means your SEO investment actively subsidises your advertising spend.

SEO also builds remarketing audiences. Organic visitors who have already engaged with your content before seeing a retargeting ad convert at significantly higher rates than cold ad audiences. Boomy Marketing connects your SEO and paid media data so remarketing lists are continuously enriched by high-intent organic visitors, improving paid ad efficiency across every campaign.
