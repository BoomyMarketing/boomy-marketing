Is National or Local SEO Better for Canadian Businesses?

Most Canadian businesses need both, with the right balance determined by their service area and business model. A Toronto accounting firm serving clients across the GTA needs aggressive local SEO. A SaaS company selling nationally needs national and even international SEO. A chain of franchises needs a coordinated combination of national brand authority and hyper-local landing pages for each location.

Boomy Marketing assesses your geographic reach during the initial strategy session and builds a campaign architecture that addresses the right level of geographic targeting.
