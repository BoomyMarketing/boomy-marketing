PUBLIC ANSWERS

Eight questions, eight prices.

Is the published price really the final price?+

For the standard Calgary window (36 × 60 in), yes. Larger or non-rectangular windows scale linearly — the calculator handles it. Final invoices have been within $11 of the locked-in price across 2,418 jobs.

How can you be 28–47% cheaper?+

We don't run a showroom, we don't pay sales commissions, and we run a single 11-person team — not a dealer network. The savings go to the customer instead of into rent and commission.

Why does Hunter Douglas not publish?+

Because they sell through dealers. Dealers set local prices. Publishing nationally would undercut their channel. We are the dealer + the manufacturer + the installer, so there is no margin to hide.

What about the comparison data?+

We obtained four anonymised Calgary quotes between Sep 2024 and Apr 2026 on the same 36 × 60 window with the same product spec. The average sits in the "Calgary AVG" column. Full methodology available on request.

How long is a price valid?+

14 days from the calculator submission. Fabric prices fluctuate; we hold the number for two weeks at our risk.

Do you do commercial?+

Yes — under 80 windows. Same published prices, dedicated installer, commercial invoicing. Boutique hotels, restaurants, dental, executive.

Custom shapes?+

Yes. Triangular, octagonal, arched. We quote within 24 hours after measurement. Add 18% on the calculator base.

Do you sell brands other than your own?+

No. Every SunLeo product is manufactured to our spec by partners in Quebec and Belgium. We never resell. We never markup.
