How much do custom blinds cost in Calgary?▼

A typical 2,400 sq ft Calgary home (12–16 windows) lands between $2,400 and $11,400 depending on style and motorization. We provide a free, line-item quote within 24 hours of your in-home visit.

How long until installation?▼

5–8 business days for standard collections, 10–12 days for custom drapery. We confirm an install date the same day you approve your quote.

What areas do you serve?▼

All of Calgary, plus Airdrie, Cochrane, Okotoks, Chestermere, Strathmore, Bearspaw, High River, Bragg Creek & surrounding municipalities within 60 km. No travel charge.

Do you remove old blinds?▼

Yes — removal and recycling are included with every install at no extra cost.

What's your warranty?▼

Lifetime, transferable. We've replaced 11-year-old mechanisms free of charge — same week.

Are your products cordless & child-safe?▼

Every product ships cordless or motorized by default. WCMA-certified child-safe.

Do you have a showroom?▼

Yes — 1217 9 Ave SE, Inglewood. Tue–Sat 10–6. Free street parking. Drop by, see & touch every fabric in actual Calgary light.
