§ 11 · Frequently asked

Before you build.

Does the configurator price match the final quote?+

Yes — within a 2% tolerance. The final measure verifies window dimensions; the price doesn't move unless the spec changes.

Lead time from order to install?+

8–10 business days for stock fabrics, 4 weeks for hardwood shutters.

Are the blinds child-safe?+

Cordless by default. Motorized has no exposed cords. ANSI/WCMA A100.1-2018 certified.

Warranty?+

Lifetime mechanism · 10 yr fabric · 5 yr motor. Calgary service techs.

Do you carry blackout liners for nurseries?+

Yes — 100% room-darkening with side channels available. Popular for shift workers and infants.

Can I save my configuration?+

Yes — email it to yourself, print as PDF, or share with a partner. Saved builds expire after 30 days.

How quiet are the motors?+

38 dB — quieter than a library whisper at 1 m.

Financing?+

Affirm, Klarna, Pay in 4. 0% APR up to 24 months on orders over $1,500.
