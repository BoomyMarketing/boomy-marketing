SunLeo · Calgary · Homepage exploration

25 design directions.

Each direction is a complete, standalone homepage proposal for SunLeo — Calgary's custom blinds & shades brand. 01–10 are preview-scale concept directions. 11–17 are full TWOPAGES-depth e-commerce builds. 18, 20 & 21 are live & animated builds with GSAP, Lenis, CSS 3D, and CRO triggers wired live. 23–27 are conversion-first directions — each one built around a single load-bearing mechanic (Quiz Funnel, Founder Story, Price Transparency, Before/After Slider, Calgary Map). Click any card to open the full mockup in a new tab.

— Volume IV · Conversion-First

Five mechanic-driven homepages.

Each of these five designs leads with one specific strategic mechanic — the one thing that does the lead-conversion heavy lifting. 23 captures qualified leads via a 6-question editorial Light Audit quiz with same-day designer report. 24 earns trust through founder portrait + a 24-photo Calgary install wall (real names, real districts, real dates). 25 publishes every starting price on the homepage with a live calculator + a public Hunter Douglas / Home Depot comparison. 26 uses 7 real draggable before/after sliders (hero + 6 vertical scroll scenes). 27 is a hand-drawn cartography SVG of Calgary with 24 pulsing pins, click-to-read install stories, and a 10-row pin log.
