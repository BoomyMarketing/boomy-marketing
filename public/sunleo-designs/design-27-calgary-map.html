— Map answers

Eight pin questions.

Is the map really live?+

Every install becomes a pin within 48 hours. Pins from this month are red; older are navy. Iris updates the map personally on Sunday evenings.

Will my address appear on it?+

Only the district and first name. Pin coordinates are anonymised to 200-metre cells. If you'd rather not appear at all, tell us during measurement and we will skip you.

What about the install story?+

Each pin story is written by Iris, after the install, with a paragraph about the room, the fabric, and the one detail that made the project memorable. You see it before it goes live.

Where do you deliver?+

Greater Calgary, Airdrie, Cochrane, Okotoks, Chestermere. Outside that, we partner with the closest atelier in our trade network.

Do you do commercial?+

Yes, under 80 windows. Boutique hotels, restaurants, dental offices, executive boardrooms. These appear as a separate pin colour on the map (yellow).

What's the guarantee?+

Ten-year atelier guarantee on craft, fit, and your peace of mind. Lifetime on hardware. Eight clients asked for a remake last year — we did them all.

How quickly is a pin added?+

Same day as install. Stories follow within the week. You receive an email with your pin link the morning after.

Who is Iris?+

Iris Laframboise. NCIDQ-certified. Born in Trois-Rivières, Calgary since 2014. Started SunLeo in 2017. Has personally pinned 3,400 windows. She also updates this map.
