— Our Story —

Born in Inglewood.
Built for the West.

Leo Aban started SunLeo in 2017 from a garage on 9th Avenue. Eight years on, our crew — most of us born & raised Calgarians — has fitted blinds in over 2,400 homes from Bearspaw to Bragg Creek.

We don't have a showroom. We bring it to your kitchen table. We don't subcontract. The person you meet is the person who installs. And we don't take shortcuts on a chinook-prone climate that eats lesser hardware alive.
