Is the sale price locked at reservation?+

Yes — once you reserve your free measure during the sale window, the 20% discount is locked even if you don't order for another 60 days. We honour the price you saw at reservation, period.

How much for a typical Calgary home?+

12–16 windows in a 2,400 sq ft home: $2,800–$11,400. With the 20% sale: $2,240–$9,120. Use the live calculator above for your real number in 30 seconds.

How fast is installation?+

5–8 business days for standard collections, 10–12 days for custom drapery. Install date confirmed the same day you approve the quote.

Why only 3 install slots this week?+

Two reasons: 1) we keep our installer teams small on purpose (better quality), and 2) the spring sale always books out fast. We currently have 3 install slots open Mon 27 → Fri 31 May. Next week opens with 7 slots.

Free measure — really $0?+

Really. No deposit, no obligation, no hidden upsell. 60-minute visit, written quote within 24h, you decide.

What's your warranty?+

Lifetime, transferable. We've replaced cordless mechanisms on 11-year-old installs free of charge, same week.

Affirm 0% — what's the catch?+

No catch — Affirm pays SunLeo upfront, you pay Affirm in 4 or 24 installments at 0% APR (for qualified buyers). Pre-qualify in 30 sec, no impact to credit score.

Do you serve Cochrane, Airdrie, Okotoks?+

Yes, plus Bearspaw, Springbank, Chestermere, Strathmore, High River. No travel charge within 60 km of downtown.
