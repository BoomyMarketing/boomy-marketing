// 11 · /docs

Frequently raised tickets.

More in our public docs at /docs. Or ping @sunleo on Discord, replies usually under 30 min.

Does it really work without internet?

Yes — Thread mesh is fully local. Scenes, schedules, and voice control via local Apple/Google hubs all work offline. Cloud is only for OTA firmware and remote-away control.

Will it work with Home Assistant?

Yes, two ways — via Matter Thread (zero-config) or our local REST/MQTT API. We publish YAML examples and a HACS integration. Both are stable.

How much for a typical Calgary home?

12–16 motorised windows in a 2,400 sq ft home: $5,400–$13,800 installed, including firmware setup, integration, and a 30-min user training session.

Battery vs hardwired?

Battery (7-year Li-ion) is standard. Hardwired option available pre-rough-in for new builds — talk to our trade desk.

How loud are the motors really?

28 dB at 1m, measured in our anechoic-foam test cabinet. Quieter than a library whisper. We have demo units at the Inglewood lab — book a hands-on session.

What's the firmware update policy?

Signed OTA updates from our public release channel. Every release is documented in the changelog at /docs/firmware. You can pin/rollback any version. Always.

Cordless & child-safe?

All motorised & cordless options are WCMA-certified child-safe. The standard offering, not an upcharge.

Warranty?

Lifetime on hardware, fabric, motor & firmware compatibility. Transferable to new homeowners. 100% of repairs done in-house in Inglewood — never sub-contracted.
