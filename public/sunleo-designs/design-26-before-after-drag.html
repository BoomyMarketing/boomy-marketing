Are these photos really real?+

Every single one. Same camera, same time of day, same lens. The only edit we do is colour correction. We will not stage a before — that defeats the point.

Can you do my home next?+

Yes — and we will photograph the before during the measurement visit, free, and hand you the after-photo on install day. Many clients use the pair on their own social media.

How long is a measurement?+

30–45 minutes for a typical home. Free, no deposit. We arrive with samples, a laser, and a camera.

How long is an install?+

One morning for a typical 8-window home. 9 AM to noon. Two installers. No mess.

Yes, under 80 windows. Boutique hotels, restaurants, dental, executive boardrooms.

Calgary only?+
