— Letter from the Founder · page 02

On taking our time.

— Leo Aban, Founder & Principal

A window is not an opening. It is a conversation between a room and the day. We've spent eight Calgary years answering that conversation slowly — one home, one north-light, one south-window at a time. This is our quarterly: a record of what we've found, who we've fitted, and why we still believe in a small studio.

— Feature · page 03

The Catalogue.

Ten window-treatment systems, photographed in the homes of clients across Calgary.

01

Roller Blinds.

— An essay on modern restraint.

The most quietly versatile of all treatments. From sheer to total blackout, from the kitchen to the cinema room. The roller is the white shirt of the window — never wrong.

02

Zebra Shades.

Translucent and opaque bands in conversation.

No showroom. We bring a fabric trunk to your house. The decision is made in the very light you live in, not in fluorescent retail.

One pair of hands.

The person who measures is the person who installs. There is no telephone game between sales and trade. Spec-grade components. The same head-rails specified by architects on tower projects.

A lifetime mechanism warranty, drafted in plain English. Honest scope, honest pricing.
