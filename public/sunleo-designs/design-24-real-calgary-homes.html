— Honest answers

Eight questions Iris hears most.

Is the in-home visit really free?+

Entirely. No deposit, no obligation, no sales follow-up. Iris drives over, measures, shows fabrics, leaves a written estimate, and waits for you to decide.

Are these really real photos?+

Yes — every photo on the Calgary wall is a SunLeo install. We do not use stock imagery. You can usually identify the neighbourhood from the window framing.

Why do you only work in Calgary?+

Because we install ourselves. Iris's crew drives. We cover Greater Calgary, Airdrie, Cochrane, Okotoks, Chestermere — anywhere we can be back by dinner.

Who is Iris, really?+

Iris Laframboise. Born in Trois-Rivières, moved to Calgary in 2014. NCIDQ-certified. Started SunLeo in 2017 with one ladder and a Subaru. Now: 3,400 installs, two installers, one designer, one ladder.

How long is a typical install?+

One morning, for a typical 8-window home. Bigger projects spread across one to two days. The crew arrives at 9 AM and leaves by noon.

Do you do commercial?+

Boutique hotels, restaurants, dental offices, executive boardrooms. Anything under 80 windows. Larger projects we refer to our trade network.

What if I hate it after install?+

We re-make it. Ten-year atelier guarantee covers craft, fit, and peace of mind. Eight clients asked for a remake last year — we did them all.

Can I just buy without the visit?+

Yes — online checkout works for stock sizes. Custom orders need a measurement. We'd rather come over once than ship the wrong size twice.
