§ 12 · Frequently asked

Before you book.

How long from measure to install?+

For in-stock fabrics, 8–10 business days. Custom shutters, 4 weeks. We never quote a date we cannot beat.

Do you really measure for free?+

Yes — within 50 km of downtown Calgary. We bring the swatch library, measure every window, and email a written quote within 24 hours.

Are SunLeo blinds child-safe?+

All cellular and roller blinds ship cordless by default. Motorized options have no exposed cords. Certified to ANSI/WCMA A100.1-2018.

What's the warranty?+

Lifetime on mechanisms. 10 years on fabrics. 5 years on motors. Calgary-based service techs, no shipping returns.

Do you accept Affirm and Klarna?+

Both, plus Pay in 4 and 0% APR plans up to 24 months on orders over $1,500.

Can I see fabrics before deciding?+

Yes — request a free swatch kit with up to 12 samples mailed in 3 business days, or visit our 17 Ave SE showroom Tue–Sat.

How quiet are the motors really?+

38 dB — quieter than a library whisper. Tested at 1 m from a bedroom shade.

What if I move or measurements change?+

We re-fit at 30% the cost of new. Most Calgary moves stay within our 50-km radius.
