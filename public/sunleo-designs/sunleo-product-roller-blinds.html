Product Details

Why Choose Custom Roller Blinds?

Unlike off-the-shelf blinds that require trimming and leave gaps, SunLeo roller blinds are made-to-measure for a perfect fit in every window. Our expert measures each opening to the millimetre — because every home is different.

Whether you need complete blackout for a bedroom, a solar screen to reduce glare in your home office, or a sheer fabric to maintain your view — we carry the right solution. All fabrics are sourced from premium suppliers and are available in Calgary within 2–3 weeks.

Every installation is handled by our certified team. We mount, level, and test every blind before we leave — so you get it right the first time.
