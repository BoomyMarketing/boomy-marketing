A founder's note

From the
Inglewood
atelier.

Iris Laframboise
Founder & Lead Designer

11 yrs dressing Calgary windows · NCIDQ certified · Mother of two, Westhaven resident.

In 2017, I rented a 400-square-foot bay on 9 Ave SE and bought one sewing machine. We had three orders the first month — and two of them were from neighbours.

Nine years later we've dressed 3,427 Calgary homes. Same address. Same machine (and four more). Iris still signs every order; that part hasn't changed.

We're independent, family-owned, and stubbornly Calgary-made. Every blind, shade, and drapery panel that leaves our shop has been touched by hand at least twice — once when we cut it, once when we hang it on your wall.

There is no Hunter Douglas dealer above me, no franchise behind me, no installer subcontracted under me. Just us. Just Calgary.

— Iris

Honest answers

Frequently asked.

Or email iris@sunleo.ca — yes, the founder, yes, she actually reads them.

How much for a typical Calgary home?+

12–16 windows in a 2,400 sq ft home lands between $4,200 and $11,400. Bedroom-only refresh starts at $890. Free written quote within 24 h of your in-home visit, no signature required.

What areas do you serve?+

All of Calgary plus Cochrane, Airdrie, Okotoks, Bearspaw, Springbank, Chestermere, Strathmore, High River, Bragg Creek — anywhere within 60 km of downtown. No travel fee, ever.

Are you really independently owned?+

Yes — Iris is the sole owner since founding in 2017. No outside investors, no franchise, no licensing. Same address, same family.

How fast can you install?+

5–8 business days for standard collections; 10–12 days for custom drapery. Install date confirmed the same day you approve the quote.

What's your warranty?+

Lifetime, transferable to new homeowners. We've replaced 11-year-old cordless mechanisms free of charge — same week.

Do you remove our old blinds?+

Yes — removal and recycling are included with every install, at no extra cost. We patch screw holes and leave each room cleaner than we found it.

Cordless & child-safe?+

Cordless or motorised by default on every product. WCMA-certified child-safe — standard, not an upcharge.

Where's your shop?+

1217 9 Ave SE in Inglewood — between Without Papers Pizza and the river path. Free street parking after 6, paid lot on the corner. Tue–Sat 10–6. Drop in, see & touch every fabric.
