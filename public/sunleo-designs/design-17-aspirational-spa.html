In case you're wondering

Quietly asked.

Or write to iris@sunleo.ca — replies usually within 12 minutes during business hours, longer on weekends.

How much for a typical Calgary home?+

A 12–16 window, 2,400 sq ft Calgary home lands between $4,200 and $11,400. A bedroom-only refresh begins at $890. Written, itemised quote within 24 h of your in-home visit, no signature needed.

How long from measure to install?+

Five to eight business days for our standard collections. Ten to twelve days for custom drapery. Install date confirmed the same day you approve your quote.

What's your warranty?+

Lifetime, transferable to new homeowners. We've replaced cordless mechanisms on 11-year-old installations free of charge, same week.

Are your fabrics natural?+

Predominantly — Belgian linen, Italian cotton, Scottish wool, Pyrenees silk. We use synthetics only where they make sense (blackouts, motorised tubes), always OEKO-TEX certified.

Do you serve Cochrane, Airdrie, Okotoks?+

Yes — across Treaty 7 territory, within 60 km of downtown Calgary. No travel surcharge, ever.

Smart home compatible?+

Matter 1.4 native — HomeKit, Google, Alexa, SmartThings, Home Assistant all included. No bridges required.

Where is your studio?+

1217 9 Ave SE, Inglewood. Between Without Papers Pizza and the river path. Tea on us, swatches in your hand, Iris usually somewhere with thread between her fingers.
