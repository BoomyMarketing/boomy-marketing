Quietly asked questions

The honest answers,
in plain English.

Twenty-three minutes of frequently asked things, condensed into nine paragraphs. Still curious? Email iris@sunleo.ca.

How much does a whole-home window treatment cost in Calgary? +

A typical 2,400 sq ft Calgary home (12–16 windows) lands between $4,200 and $11,400 depending on fabric tier, motorisation, and layering. A bedroom-only refresh starts around $890. We provide written, line-item quotes within 24 hours of your in-home visit — no signature required.

How long from measure to installation? +

5–8 business days for in-stock collections. 10–12 days for custom drapery. We confirm an install date the same day you approve your quote.

Do you serve Cochrane, Airdrie, Okotoks? +

Yes — all surrounding municipalities including Bearspaw, Springbank, Chestermere, Strathmore, and High River. No travel surcharge within 60 km of downtown.

What's your warranty? +

Lifetime, transferable to new homeowners. We've replaced cordless mechanisms on 11-year-old installations free of charge — same week.

Can I see fabrics before committing? +

Always. Order 8 free swatches online, or visit the Inglewood atelier (1217 9 Ave SE) Tuesday–Saturday. We never ask anyone to choose a fabric from a screen.

Are your motorised shades compatible with Apple HomeKit? +

Yes, via Matter or a Lutron Caséta hub. Google Home, Alexa, and Samsung SmartThings also supported out of the box.

Do you remove old blinds during installation? +

Yes, removal & recycling are included with every install — at no charge. We patch screw holes and leave each room cleaner than we found it.

Where is your showroom? +

1217 9 Ave SE, Inglewood — between Without Papers Pizza and the river path. Free street parking after 6, paid lot at the corner. Coffee is on us; Iris is usually there.
