§ 04 · The unhurried process

Three quiet steps to a softer room.

The visit

A specialist arrives with 200 swatches, a printed lookbook, and a cup of tea. We sit on your sofa, watch the light, and sketch by hand. The visit lasts an hour. The quote is printed before we leave.

The atelier

Twelve makers on 17 Avenue cut, stitch, and quality-check your treatments. Eight business days, no rush. You'll receive a photograph of your shades on the bench before they ship.

The install

A two-person crew arrives in clean shoes, drop cloths, and a small vacuum. We test every cord, motor, and pairing. A one-year tune-up visit is included, no charge.

§ 12 · Frequently asked

Before your quiet visit.

How long from visit to install?+

Eight to ten business days for in-stock fabrics; four weeks for hardwood shutters. We never quote a date we cannot beat.

Is the visit truly free?+

Yes — within 50 km of downtown Calgary. We bring 200 swatches and a printed quote.

Are the treatments child-safe?+

Cordless by default. Motorized has no exposed cords. ANSI/WCMA A100.1-2018 certified.

What's the warranty?+

Lifetime mechanism · ten years on fabrics · five years on motors. Calgary-based techs, no shipping returns.

Can I see fabrics first?+

A free swatch kit of twelve samples arrives in three business days, or visit our 17 Avenue atelier Tue–Sat.

How quiet are the motors?+

38 dB — quieter than a library whisper, measured at one metre from a bedroom shade.

Do you do nurseries and bedrooms?+

Yes — blackout liners, side channels, and motorized schedules for shift workers and new parents.

Financing?+

Affirm, Klarna, Pay in 4, and 0% APR up to 24 months on orders over $1,500.
