— Quiet answers

Eight honest questions.

Is the audit really free?+

Entirely. There is no follow-up sales call unless you ask for one. You will receive a single, designer-written report by email. That is the whole offer.

What happens after I submit?+

Iris reads every audit personally. Within four hours she emails three product recommendations, one Calgary install reference, and an installed-cost estimate. You decide what to do next.

Do you actually publish prices?+

Starting prices, yes — they are on every product card and on the comparison page. Final pricing comes after measurement, because every window is custom. Your audit budget caps the recommendations.

Where do you deliver?+

Greater Calgary, Airdrie, Cochrane, Okotoks, Chestermere. Outside that radius, we partner with the closest atelier in our trade network.

What if I hate the product after install?+

We re-make it. The 10-year atelier guarantee covers craft, fit, and your peace of mind. You will not be the first to ask for a remake — eight clients did last year.

Will you call me?+

Only if you ask. Email is the default. We are a small atelier and we respect quiet inboxes.

Do you do commercial?+

Boutique hotels, restaurants, dental offices, executive boardrooms. Anything under 80 windows. For larger projects we refer to our trade partners.

Who is Iris?+

Iris Laframboise is the atelier's founder and only designer. NCIDQ-certified. Eleven years in Calgary. Has dressed 3,400 windows since 2017. She also reads every audit, personally.
