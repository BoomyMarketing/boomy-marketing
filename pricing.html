Pricing FAQs

What is the minimum commitment? +

We ask for an initial 3-month commitment on all plans to give strategies enough time to show results. After that, it's month-to-month with 30 days notice to cancel. We don't believe in locking clients into long contracts — we keep you because of results.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +

Yes — you can upgrade at any time and the change takes effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. We'll always recommend the right plan for your current stage of growth.

Do you charge a setup fee? +

No setup fees on any plan. The first month includes onboarding — account setup, tracking installation, strategy development, and access to your client dashboard. Everything is included in your monthly rate from day one.

What does "AI development included" mean on the Scale plan? +

Scale clients receive 10 hours/month of custom AI development from our Dev K. and AI team. This covers building custom workflows, LLM integrations, voice agents, AI chatbots, or any other AI tool specific to your business needs. Additional hours can be purchased at $185 CAD/hr.

Do prices include ad spend? +

No — management fees are separate from ad spend. Your ad budget goes directly to Google or Meta; we manage the campaigns on top of that. We recommend minimum $1,500 CAD/mo in ad spend for Google Ads and $1,000 CAD/mo for Meta Ads to see meaningful results.
